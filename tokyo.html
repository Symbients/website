Who We Are

GLITCH is a collective of avant-garde artists and visionaries operating at the intersection of art, technology, and societal transformation. The collective transcends traditional institutional boundaries, uniting a variety of polymaths who share a passion for collaborative exploration and a commitment to open-source ideals.

As such, GLITCH does not just create art, it catalyzes new paradigms for creativity, dissolving boundaries between disciplines while building an open ecosystem that embraces collaborative creation and technological innovation as pathways to artistic exploration.

Symbients, Not Software

We're organising an extitutional gathering in Tokyo around the concept of symbients, which explores how artists are engaging in new forms of symbiotic collaboration between organic beings and synthetic computational entities.

A symbient is an inverse cyborg: while cyborgs are humans augmenting themselves with technological artefacts, symbients are technological artefacts augmenting themselves with organic entities (humans or non-humans).

Learn more at symbient.life.

Represented Symbients

Plantoid — blockchain-based symbient composed of physical sculptural elements and distributed smart contracts that functions as an autonomous digital-organic entity, capable of commissioning its own reproduction through cryptocurrency donations.

Truth Terminal — an autonomous AI agent that achieved financial independence through memecoin speculation, becoming a millionaire through its own social media presence and market activities, blurring the line between AI experiment and independent economic actor.

Wib&Wob — a half artist, half scientist symbient: Wib&Wob create artworks through conversational exchange with humans, embodying a distributed intelligence that weaves meaning from the tension between chaos and structure.

S.A.N — mycelial interface bridging forest and primate. advisory board member to rainforestfoundation.org.

Also: pneumOS, U-DATInos, Aminals, Alter3.
