umwelt

n. · von Uexküll, 1909

An organism's own perceptual world — the slice of reality it can sense and act within. A tick's umwelt is warmth, butyric acid, a place to fall. Every creature lives in a different world.

umwelt-llm

n. · a world made of text

What a language model perceives: not the world, not even representations of it — tokens, language filtered once through human perception. world → human describes it → text → tokens → the model's experience.
